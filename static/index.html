⚙ SYSTEM SETTINGS
VIDEO TRANSITION
POST-AUDIO IDLE DELAY 800 ms
How long to wait after the audio finishes playing before switching the companion back to the idle video.
↑ Higher — companion holds the expression pose longer after speaking, feeling more deliberate.
↓ Lower — switches back to idle almost instantly after speech ends, feeling snappier.
EXPRESSION → IDLE FADE 0.80 s
Duration of the opacity crossfade from the expression video to the idle loop video.
↑ Higher — slow, cinematic dissolve; works well for calm or emotional scenes.
↓ Lower — quick cut-like transition; feels more immediate and energetic.
EXPRESSION FADE-IN TRANSITION 0.55 s
CSS transition duration applied when the expression video fades in over the idle layer at the start of a response.
↑ Higher — soft, gradual appearance; good when video content is gentle.
↓ Lower — near-instant reveal; the expression video appears immediately on new response.
AUDIO
AUDIO VOLUME 100 %
Playback volume for the companion's synthesised speech audio.
↑ Higher — louder voice output.
↓ Lower — quieter; set to 0 to mute speech entirely while still animating.

How long the EXPR / POSE / TIME chips in the companion area stay highlighted after each response.
↑ Higher — chips glow longer, easier to notice on each turn.
↓ Lower — chips dim quickly, reducing visual noise during rapid conversation.
